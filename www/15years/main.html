Celebrating 15 Years of BioQuant

Welcome to BioQuant, the Center for Quantitative Analysis of Molecular and Cellular Biosystems!

BioQuant comprises three pillars: quantitative experimentation, modern technologies, and computation. Together our scientists study complex biological systems to uncover their fundamental principles. We also develop innovative technology platforms and provide them to the scientific community.

Founded in 2007, our institute has served to advance biomedical research and education. Since then we have grown into a hub of the multidisciplinary research network.

And this year we are celebrating BioQuant's 15th anniversary.

To mark the occasion, we are hosting a scientific symposium and a jubilee party. We cordially invite all current and former members of BioQuant to join us on Tuesday, June 21, 2022.
