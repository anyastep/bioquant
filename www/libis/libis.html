Baden-Württemberg Institute for Bioinformatics Infrastructure

LIBIS provides bioinformatics software, hardware and cloud computing facilities for the scientific community. We integrate services of the German Network in Bioinformatics in Baden-Württemberg.

We are funded and supported by the Federal Ministry of Education and Research, Klaus Tschira Foundation, Heidelberg University and de.NBI.

Modern bioinformatics tools for your research

We develop resources for DNA, RNA and omics bioinformatics as well as for high-throughput data management and analysis. We also offer modeling tools, workflows and cloud computing services.
